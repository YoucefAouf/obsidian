Given the foot position $p^W_f$, we calculate the next foothold we calculate $p^W_{f,d}$ for each leg as following:
$$\begin{align}
    x^W_{FR,d} &= x^W_{FL} + \frac{l}{2} \\
    x^W_{FL,d} &= x^W_{RL} + L\\
    x^W_{RR,d} &= x^W_{RL}+ \frac{l}{2}\\
    x^W_{RL,d} &= x^W_{RL} + l
\end{align}$$
With $l$ the step length and $L$ the body length (distance between front and rear hips).
For simplicity, we keep $y$ and $z$ the same:
$$\begin{align}
    y^W_{f,d} &= y^W_{f} \\
    z^W_{f,d} &= z^W_{f}
\end{align}$$
